# Reference Timeline

This file is the human-readable historical anchor for the sandbox.

## 1941

- June 1941: Germany launches Barbarossa. This is a major decision window and the first serious slice for the sandbox.
- December 1941: The German advance before Moscow stalls and the Soviet counteroffensive shows that the campaign will not resolve quickly.

## 1942-1943

- November 1942 to February 1943: Stalingrad becomes the decisive disaster case for force-preservation and leadership-friction testing.
- May 1943: U-boat losses spike and the Atlantic campaign shifts structurally against Germany.

## 1944-1945

- June 1944: D-Day opens the western lodgment.
- August 1944: Romania defects, damaging German oil security.
- Early 1945: The Reich collapses under converging military, economic, and political pressure.
